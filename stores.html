Shipping and Return Policy:

Shipping Policy:

We strive to process and ship all orders within 1-3 business

days (excluding weekends and holidays) from the date of purchase. Please

note that processing times may vary during peak seasons or promotional periods.

We offer various shipping options to cater to your needs. At checkout, you will

be able to choose from standard shipping, expedited shipping, or any other available options.

Return Policy:

If for any reason you are not, we accept returns within 30 days from the date of delivery.

To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in

the same condition as received.To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team.

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size,color,or style, please contactour customer

support team.If ever you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer

support team immediately.We'll arrange a replacement or offer a refund, including shipping costs.
